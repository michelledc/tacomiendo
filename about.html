our story

As California Latinas from Mexican ancestry we thought that since this land was once ruled by our great great great (etc.) grandparents, we might find good tacos anywhere in the country. Living in the DMV has proved otherwise. Therefore we set out, along with our Argentine partner in crime, to find a place where all Mexicans (and non-Mexicans) can enjoy a good taco.

TACO + COMIENDO

Taco (duh) + the Spanish word for "eating"
