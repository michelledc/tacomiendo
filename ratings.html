Our Rating System

We rate tacos based on their quality and taste, but most importantly by their authenticity. As stated in our mission, we are in search of true Mexican taco – none of that Taco Bell stuff (disclaimer: La Borracha is a fan of said establishment). Below you'll find a breakdown of our rating system!

"A toda madre"

It's off the hook!

This taco has completely hit the spot (that Mexican stomach spot!) This is the most authentic taco you can find. It has a the savory meat, and the greasy tortilla topped off with just the right amount of cebolla and cilantro with some salsa verde o roja. It has some limonsito, cebollitas, grilled chile verde and rabanitos on the side.

"Esta chido"

It's cool

This means that this taco is good OR maybe even great...but not 100% Mexicano. In other words my tia in Mexico would probably not be too fond of it but you can find me grubbing on one from time to time. It has the right idea, but is missing an essential detail!

"Ni de Aqui Ni de Alla"

It's good, but not Mexican

Most likely fusion, oh pocho, como dicen in the southern border. The taste of this taco can be hit or miss. It probably has the hipster vibe, with healthier tortilla and veggie options, and some sriracha sauce, which could be great if you’re on a diet or want to look cool if you’re into that kind of thing.

"Que Gacho Gabacho"

It's... meh

I barely recognize this as a taco. It’s a flour tortilla and abundant veggies makes it almost like a burrito in disguise. It lacks vibrant color, and most importantly flavor!
